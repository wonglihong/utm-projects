On December 18th 2025, I was once again given the golden opportunity by the Faculty of Computing to attend another industry talk titled "Project Management and System Development". The talk was offered by Ts. Hj. Abdul Alim bin Abdul Muttalib, the Head of Technology and Innovation department of Serunai Commerce Sdn. Bhd. The main highlight of this talk was the basic skills of project management and system development.

- üî• Mr. Abdul first shared us the definitions and approaches of system development life cycle, a fundamental methodology used to create, design and manage information systems. He highlighted the strengths and weaknesses of both approaches whilst giving us a practical example to demonstrate the use cases of both methods. He best summarises the importance of SDLC in one quote: "If you fail to plan, you plan to fail".
- ü§ñ Then, Mr. Abdul introduced us to agentic coding and empathised its importance in today's world. He recommended us to "stop being a 'coder' and start being a 'system architect', managing the AI to build the system for us".
